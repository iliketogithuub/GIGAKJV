## IF YOU STILL REJECT THIS TRUTH

You've just read:

- Scripture after scripture proving healing is in the atonement
- Hebrew word studies showing choliy and makob ALWAYS mean physical sickness
- Greek word analysis confirming the New Testament interpretation
- Historical evidence from AD 100-400 documenting unbroken healing ministry
- Exegetical precision on 1 Corinthians 13:8-10
- Logical demolition of every major objection
- Honest treatment of difficult questions

If after all this evidence you still reject divine health, it's not because of lack of proof.

It's because of something else.

**THE SCRIPTURE PAUL WARNED ABOUT**

2 Timothy 3:7 (NKJV): "Always learning and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth."

There are people who study endlessly but never arrive at truth. Not because truth is unavailable. But because they refuse to accept it.

They're like the Pharisees Jesus confronted:

John 5:39-40 (NKJV): "You search the Scriptures, for in them you think you have eternal life; and these are they which testify of Me. But you are not willing to come to Me that you may have life."

They had the Scriptures. They studied them. But they rejected the Truth standing right in front of them.

**THE WARNING**

2 Timothy 3:2-5 (NKJV): "For men will be lovers of themselves, lovers of money, boasters, proud, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy, unloving, unforgiving, slanderers, without self-control, brutal, despisers of good, traitors, headstrong, haughty, lovers of pleasure rather than lovers of God, having a form of godliness but denying its power. And from such people turn away!"

Read that last line again: "Having a form of godliness but DENYING ITS POWER."

That's cessationism in one sentence.

They have church services. They read the Bible. They pray prayers. They have a FORM of godliness.

But they DENY THE POWER.

They deny:
- The power to heal
- The power to deliver
- The power to cast out demons
- The power to demonstrate the Kingdom

And God's instruction? "FROM SUCH TURN AWAY."

**THE CHOICE**

You now have a choice.

**OPTION 1: Accept the Truth**

Believe what the Scripture says:
- Isaiah 53:4-5 - "By His stripes we are healed"
- 1 Peter 2:24 - "By whose stripes you were healed"
- Matthew 8:17 - "He Himself took our infirmities and bore our sicknesses"

Receive your healing. Walk in divine health. Minister to others.

**OPTION 2: Reject the Truth**

Hold on to your theological tradition that denies God's power.

Explain away the Greek and Hebrew.
Ignore the historical evidence.
Dismiss 2,000 years of documented miracles.
Accept sickness as "God's will."

And one day stand before Jesus and explain why you:
- Had His Word but didn't believe it
- Had the evidence but rejected it
- Had the power but denied it
- Had the truth but taught against it

**THE FINAL WORD**

Romans 3:4 (NKJV): "Let God be true but every man a liar."

Your theological tradition can be wrong. Church history can be wrong. Seminary professors can be wrong. Denominations can be wrong.

But God's Word is NEVER wrong.

The Word says: "By His stripes you were healed."

That's past tense. Finished. Done. Yours.

If you reject that, you're not rejecting me. You're not rejecting this book. You're rejecting the Word of God.

**HOSEA 4:6 - THE CONCLUSION**

Hosea 4:6 (NKJV): "My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge. Because you have rejected knowledge, I also will reject you from being priest for Me; because you have forgotten the law of your God, I also will forget your children."

God's people are destroyed. Not because healing isn't available. But because they reject the knowledge of it.

Don't be among those destroyed for lack of knowledge.

The knowledge is here. The evidence is clear. The Word is plain.

**Your move.**
